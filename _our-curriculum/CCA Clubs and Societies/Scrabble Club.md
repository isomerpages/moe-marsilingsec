<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>To nurture scrabble players who:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>are competent in scrabble competitive board play through sustained participation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>demonstrate valuable attributes like honesty, integrity and perseverance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>exercise core values, social and emotional competencies and 21st Century
Competencies such as independent and critical thinking</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>apply multiple intelligences such as intra-inter personal intelligence,
logical reasoning, fluid and spatial intelligence</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Programmes</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Scrabble training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Friendly sparring with other schools</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Internal competitions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participation in inter-school scrabble competitions</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Achievements</strong>
</p>
<p>The Scrabble Club prides itself on equipping players with resilience,
adaptability and courage under pressure.&nbsp; These traits demonstrated
by scrabble players allowed the Scrabble Club to clinch its first Championship
title in 2012.&nbsp; To date, the Scrabble Club is an 8th&nbsp;time National
Champion at the yearly Inter-School National Scrabble Championships.</p>
